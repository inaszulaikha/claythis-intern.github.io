Claythis: Skydeck Intern Presentation

Carly Chan and Inas Zulaikha Anwar

Overview

In this project with Claythis, we focused on developing a robust 2D-to-3D character rendering pipeline using LoRA fine-tuning on Stable Diffusion models. We researched and implemented methods to generate high-fidelity 3D clay-style renderings from flat 2D images while preserving the original character's anatomy, pose, and expression. We curated and preprocessed datasets, tuned model parameters, and tested various training workflows using platforms like ComfyUI and RunPod. We also debugged LoRA training issues, optimized rendering quality, and ensured consistent output styles across prompts. This hands-on experience deepened our understanding of model fine-tuning, data-centric AI workflows, and the practical challenges of creative AI deployment in production environments.
